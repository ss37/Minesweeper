Minesweeper

How to play?

Objective: You have to find all the five mines in this grid of 5x5.

  1. The number on the top left shows the number of mines to find.
  2. The number on the top right is the timer.
  3. Click on any random square.
  4. The number shown signifies the number of mines that particular square is touching(top, bottom, sides and diagonally).
  5. If you click on a mine, it's game over.
  6. Keep playing to improve your time!

This web page works best with Google Chrome.
